Fascinating Fact!
While pigeons are believed to be dirty birds, they are actually quite clean. The risk of contracting a disease from one is quite low, and their personal hygiene is a priority. Pigeons spend most of their day grooming themselves, or otherwise find water to bathe in.
Fascinating Fact!
Not only can pigeons recongize mirror reflections and are better than humans at multi-tasking, but they also have amazing navigational skills and can return to their homes from up to 2092km away, even when transported to an area that they have never seen before. Scientists believe that they have a compass and map system. The compass is thought to be determined by the sun, while the source of their mapping skills is still unknown; a pleasant mystery..
